HOW CAN YOU HELP?
Every player — with or without the ball — can always be helping. Never watch. Always ask: how am I helping right now?
IP
In Possession
Help Your Team Attack
Create passing lanes by moving into space before the ball arrives — give the carrier an easy option. Attack defenders 1v1 to commit them, then lay off to a teammate in a better position. Hold up play to let teammates join the attack and create numerical advantages. Switch the point of attack quickly to find space on the other side. Make the field as big as possible — width and depth stretch the defense and open gaps for everyone.
OP
Out of Possession
Press · Cover · Recover · Defend
When possession is lost, react immediately — the first 5 seconds are critical. Get goalside and recover your defensive shape before the opposition can exploit the transition. Press the ball carrier as a unit to force play into predictable areas. Stay connected — gaps between players are what good teams exploit. If you are not the one pressing, cover the space your teammate has vacated. Win the second ball if the press is beaten. Defending is a team effort from front to back — strikers press, midfielders cover, defenders hold the line. Everyone has a role whether they are near the ball or not.
COACHING TIP Never watch the game. At every moment ask: How can I help right now? Help attack, help defend, help support, help spread. Every player has a job at every moment — find yours.
