THE 5 CHANNELS — In Possession
When we have the ball, we use all 5 channels to create width, open space, and attack with purpose.
W
Wide Channels
Wingers · Fullbacks · Create Width
Stretch the defense in wide channels. Pull defenders out of position to create space for teammates in the middle. Be attack-minded in 1v1 situations and look to create 2v1 opportunities with overlapping runs.
H
Half-Spaces
Forwards · Midfielders · Find Pockets
Find pockets of space between defenders. The half-space between the fullback and center-back is where danger is created. Combine with teammates, vary your runs, and look to get behind or between the defensive line.
C
Central Channel
Strikers · Central Mids · Control
Control the central channel to control the game. It's crowded — use wide play to move defenders, open passing lanes, and unlock the center. The central channel is where you finish attacks, not start them.
COACHING TIP Use all 5 channels to stretch the defense, find pockets of space, and create clear chances on goal.
