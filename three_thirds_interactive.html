THE THREE THIRDS
Every decision on the ball should be shaped by where you are on the field.
ATTACKING Be Creative · Take Risks
MIDDLE Control Tempo · Be Smart
DEFENDING Stay Compact · Be Safe
A
Attacking Third
Opponent's End · Be Creative
This is where chances turn into goals. Be brave, take risks, trust your ability, and move with purpose and creativity. Wait for a quality chance — don't force it. If you lose the ball, react immediately — press and win it back with intensity.
M
Middle Third
Center of the Field · Be Smart
The core of the game. Keep the ball moving, link up with teammates, and switch the play to exploit space. Quick, smart decisions here control the tempo of the entire game.
D
Defending Third
Your End · Be Safe
Protect your goal first — always. Play simple, safe passes and clear danger quickly. Stay aware, stay connected, and cover space. Mistakes here can cost goals, so stay calm and keep it simple.
COACHING TIP Recognize the third you're in before the ball arrives. Let that awareness dictate your next action — your first touch, your speed of play, and your level of risk.
